Why BSC ?

We chose BSC because of lower gas fees in comparison to ETH. The MetaDa team aims for this token to be community driven so the mechanisms and features of the token are designed to help with the long-term growth of the project.

How to buy?

1) Download Trust Wallet on your Mobile Device If you are using iOS, head over to the App Store and search Trust Wallet it should be the first option with the shield logo, if you are on Android, head onto the Google Play Store and again search Trust Wallet and it should be the first option with the shield logo once again.

2) Visit PancakeSwap and swap your Smart Chain BNB after you bought it from Binance or directly from Trust Wallet for $METADA, click here to go directly to PancakeSwap, or manually copy the contract address, after you've copied the address, paste it into the search bar on PancakeSwap, use WalletConnect to connect Trust Wallet if you're on iOS, set the slippage to 18%. If you get an error, increase the slippage to 20% and if you continue to get errors, round your amount to the nearest ten.

3) Check you've received your $METADA in your wallet.
If you can't see your MetaDa in your wallet, click the icon in the top-right hand corner, type in MetaDa select 'Add Custom Token'. Insert the Polaris address, write MetaDa for Name, $METADA for symbol and choose 4 Decimals, congratulations, you are now a proud holder of $METADA!

How can we get our rewards?

You can start enjoying receipt of rewards by simply buying MetaDa and holding them in your wallet. It’s that simple and easy to enjoy passive income! The more of it you hold, the bigger the rewards!
